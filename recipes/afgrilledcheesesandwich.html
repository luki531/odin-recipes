Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This delicious 'sandwich' is perfect for April Fool's Day, Halloween, or even birthday parties.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound cake, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon yellow food coloring
  • 1/8 teaspoon red food coloring

Steps:

  1. Spread butter on both sides of each pound cake slice.
  2. Heat a griddle over medium heat. Cook cakes slices on hot griddle, turning once, until golden brown on each side, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer cake slices to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. Stir frosting, yellow food coloring, and red food coloring together in a bowl until frosting is the color of American cheese.
  4. Spread frosting on 1 side of half of the pound cake slices and top with another slice of pound cake.
